Presenting an interactive data visualization dashboard focused on energy distribution, sustainability, and accessibility, aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy).
The dashboard provides insights into how different energy sources contribute to electricity generation, their environmental impact, and global access to electricity.

**Objectives:
**
Analyze the relationship between economic growth and environmental impact

Track renewable, fossil fuel, and nuclear electricity generation trends

Compare electricity access across countries

Identify top electricity-generating countries

Support policy-making and sustainable energy planning
